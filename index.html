Unified Robot Morpholgy Architecture

URMA splits the observations of a robot into joint, foot and general parts, encodes them with a simple attention encoder with a learnable temperature, and uses an universal morphology decoder to obtain the actions for every joint of the robot.

Deployment on Robots from the Training Set

After training on 16 robots in simulation with extensive domain randomization, the policy is deployed on two of them in the real world.

Deployment on Unseen Robots

Because of the big variety of robots in the training set, the randomization of their properties and the morphology-agnostic URMA architecture, the policy can generalize to real robots not seen during prior the simulation training.
